Dreamos is a resturant of fine dine and elegance specalising in European and Middle Eastern Cuisnes

OVERVIEW

History

In the early 2000 Dreamos was an inspired idea between 2 brothers who have a love and passion for food and the business industry. With their combined ideas and thoughts the brothers have sparked an intrest in starting up their own resturant chain that specialises in their favourite cuisines that originate from European and Middle Eastern cultures. It has taken 2 years of hard work and dedication towards the planning, setting and start up of the business. On the 25th of January 2003, Dreamos opened its doors for the first time to the public and ever since, Dreamos has grown exponentially catering to diners for authentic experience. Dreamos has expanded to 7 of 9 provinces in South Africa and have been delving to the International scene. The name Dreamos orignated from the brothers dreams and passion of starting up a resturant that not only grows locally but also internationally.

Vision

Our vision is to create a dining experience that is authentic yet homey. We want our diners to have that feel good experience like that of a cup of tea in the evening. Eating is not just a need, it's an absolute must have! We envision a future where we supply the best using only the finest ingredients that have been sustainably sourced.
